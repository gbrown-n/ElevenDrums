About Me

About Me

Meet Von

Hi, my name is Von, I am trained in the Drumbeat, Drum Circle and Rhythm 2 Recovery programs. I was honoured to participate in Drum Circle Facilitator training with Arthur Hull.

I have worked in the mental health field for over 20 years. I have the passion and drive to support participants through drumming and storytelling in a safe environment.

I started Eleven Drums in 2024 because I wanted to combine my passion for drumming and for mental health into a role that could really make a difference in the lives of the people in my community. This adventure is fuelled by my own blood, sweat and tears.

My Core Values

Respect

Respect comes in many forms - for self, for others and for country. It is through respect that we learn and share with others.

Welcoming

Holding space so that others can feel their feelings, be hard, express themselves and ask for help.

Authenticity

Sometimes it's difficult to hear the truth, so being authentic in a respectful manner is key to the success of the group and the learning process.

Our Origins

Why "Eleven" drums?

The name "Eleven Drums" is a reference to my drum collection, which I have accumulated over the past decade. I share these drums with our drumming groups so that everyone can participate in this wonderful experience.

This also means we facilitate for up to ten people in one session, which produces tight-nit intimate groups to share our expereinces.

The colors blue and green are also calming and associated with mindfullness and mental health. They are also the color scheme of my drums.
