Meet Von

Von is an accredited Drumbeat Facilitator, also trained in the Rhythm to Recovery program. She was honoured to participate in Drum Circle Facilitator training with Arthur Hull.

Von has worked in the mental health field for over 20 years. She has the passion and drive to support participants through drumming and storytelling in a safe environment.

Drum circles are my favourite thing to do!

Von Bottrell Founder
