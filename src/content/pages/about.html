Meet Von

Von is trained in the Drumbeat, Drum Circle and Rhythm 2 Recovery programs. She was honoured to participate in Drum Circle Facilitator training with Arthur Hull.

Von has worked in the mental health field for over 20 years. She has the passion and drive to support participants through drumming and storytelling in a safe environment.

Respect

Respect comes in many forms - for self, for others and for country. It is through respect that we learn and share with others.

Welcoming

Holding space so that others can feel their feelings, be hard, express themselves and ask for help.

Authenticity

Sometimes it's difficult to hear the truth, so being authentic in a respectful manner is key to the success of the group and the learning process.
