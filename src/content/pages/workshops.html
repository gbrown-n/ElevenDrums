Rhythm of the Soul

Theraputic Drumming Groups

Eleven Drums focuses on group drumming sessions of up to ten people. This creates intimate, close-knit groups that faciliate inclusion, connection and belonging.

Group therapy has the benefit of providing high quality interactions, at a fraction of the cost of one on one therapy.

All equipment is provided - we use my very own collection of traditional drums to facilitate our growth as a community.

Something for Everyone

Safe Spaces for All

Each session is tailored to the participants who show up, with a core theme of connection, inclusion, self-care and learning who you are.

I offer a number of different groups to provide spaces where everyone feels safe to be themselves.
