Rhythm of the Soul

Theraputic Drumming Groups

Eleven Drums focuses on group drumming sessions of up to ten people. This creates intimate, close-nit groups that faciliate inclusion, connection and belonging.

Group therapy has the benefit of providing high quality interactions, at a fraction of the cost of one on one therapy.

All equipment is provided - we use my very own collection of traditional drums to facilitate our growth as a community.
