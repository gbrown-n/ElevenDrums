Therapy Through Rhythm

Eleven Drums

At Eleven Drums, we focus on combining the benefits of group therapy and community safe spaces, with the communicative and grounding rhythms of theraputic drumming.

Based in the Lockyer Region of South East Queensland, we reach out to vulnerable persons and welcome them into one of our many drumming circles, where we work together to improve each others lives.

More About Us
Rhythm Therapy

Soothe Your Soul

Therapeutic drumming uses traditional drums as a way of communicating feelings and create a sense of inclusion, connection and belonging within a close-knit drumming circle. Drums provide a rhythm that everyone can hook into, no matter the level of experience in both drumming and therapy.

The combination of drumming, conversation, listening, and feeling included in a safe space, is calming, inspirational and healing.

Workshops

Affordable

Group therapy has the benefit of providing high quality interactions, at a fraction of the cost of one on one therapy.

Equipment Provided

All drumming equipment is provided - all you need is a willingness to learn and connect.

Tailored

Each session is tailored to the participants who show up, with a core theme of connection, inclusion, self-care and learning who you are.
